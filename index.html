About

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Toulouse INP - ENSEEIHT / IRIT (Toulouse, France).
In the end of August 2026, I am free for position in academic or indistrual research in my domain.


Previously, I was a PhD Student in the ParSys team at Laboratoire Interdisciplinaire des Sciences du Numérique (LISN), from Université Paris-Saclay.
My thesis on mixed precision for low-rank approximation to matrix and tensor was supervised by Marc Baboulin (director), Oguz Kaya, and Théo Mary.
