About

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Toulouse INP - ENSEEIHT / IRIT (Toulouse, France).


Previously, I was a member of the ParSys team at Laboratoire Interdisciplinaire des Sciences du Numérique (LISN), Université Paris-Saclay.


My research focuses on numerical linear algebra, tensor decompositions, mixed precision algorithms and high-performance computing.
